## Introduction

Bitango Technologies, s.r.o. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the **Minibits Wallet** mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the Minibits Wallet app you acknowledge this Privacy Policy. If you are not comfortable with any of the practices described here, please discontinue use of the application.

---

## Information We Collect

We collect **limited information** during app usage and do not acquire personal data from external vendors.

### Information processed by the app

- **Randomly generated wallet identifier** — a unique, anonymous identifier generated on your device.
- **Shareable wallet name and associated public key** — a human-readable name you may choose and share with others.
- **Random profile picture** — an avatar connected to your wallet identity, generated randomly.
- **Anonymous diagnostics and error reporting data** — crash reports and performance metrics that help us improve the app.

### Information stored only on your device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is **never transmitted to our servers**:

- Encryption keys created locally on your device
- eCash tokens from your selected mints
- Private keys associated with your wallet accounts
- Transaction records
- Peer contact information stored as shareable wallet names
